Punchline


Punchline is a local, up-to 4 player versus brawler, played with Xbox controllers. It is a glorious battle royal on 3 different stages for the last man standing. Utilize your unique mechanics to shove your enemies off the platform.

  • Client: Saxion (Studentproject)
  • Date: May 2018 - July 2018
  • Service: Game Development

Overcharged


Overcharged is a futuristic local network multiplayer sports game, where you can play against another player in a sci-fi ball sport. The easiest way to explain it would be "Space-Quidditch". You possess a jetpack-like fly ability, as well as a short ranged dash, in order for you to dominate the airspace and you can either reflect or catch and aim the ball, but beware ... the longer you aim the more imprecise it gets.

  • Client: Saxion (Studentproject)
  • Date: February 2018 - March 2018
  • Service: Game Development

Buccaneer's Bay


Buccaneer's Bay is an action packed pirate game, where you battle with other ships for dominance over the seas. You control a pirate ship, sailing into various dangerous missions to claim booty and to upgrade your ship. But beware of the imperial fleet and its might.

  • Client: Saxion (Studentproject)
  • Date: September 2017 - October 2017
  • Service: Game Development
